Steps To Make Chicken Biriyani 🥘🍲🍗

  1. Marinate chicken: mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic, red chilli, turmeric, biryani masala, salt, half the mint & coriander, green chillies and lemon juice — refrigerate 30 min–2 hr.
  2. Fry onions till deep golden; drain on paper — reserve ¾ for layering and ¼ for garnish.
  3. Boil water with salt + whole spices; add soaked rice and cook until ~70% done (grains still firm). Drain.
  4. In a heavy-bottom pot, heat oil/ghee, add a few whole spices, sauté 2–3 tbsp fried onions, then add marinated chicken and cook on medium until chicken is about 80% done and oil separates (10–15 min).
  5. Reduce heat. Layering: spread half the par-cooked rice over the chicken, sprinkle half the chopped mint & coriander and some fried onions.
  6. Add remaining rice as top layer; sprinkle remaining mint, coriander, saffron milk, a tablespoon ghee, and remaining fried onions.
  7. Seal pot (tight lid or dough) and dum: cook on high for 5–7 min to build steam, then very low heat for 25–30 min (or bake sealed at 180°C / 350°F for 20–25 min). Use a tawa/griddle under the pot to avoid burning.
  8. Turn off heat and let rest sealed for 10 min.
  9. Open carefully, gently fluff and mix layers once or twice to distribute chicken and rice.
  10. Serve hot with raita, salad and lemon wedges; garnish extra fried onions and coriander.
